Typhoon drum machine

A project in Overtone to recreate the Dave Smith Instruments Tempest drum machine in software.

Development

Feel free to fork and help develop it, or take it in different directions. The goal is to make this a community project, and let people build all sorts of different interfaces for it.

It helps a lot to checkout the Tempest user manual and demo videos here:

http://www.davesmithinstruments.com/products/tempest/
